Introduction

I'm passionate about financial markets, data, and the systems that drive them. With a background in applied mathematics and computer science, I bring a technical foundation to analysis blending programming, quantitative thinking, and curiosity to extract value from datasets.

My interest in analysis grew out of hands-on experience by writing code to explore historical option data. I am especially drawn to the intersection of data analysis and decision-making: how numbers guide strategy and turn to action. Currently, I am pursuing professional certification in the finance industry to complement my mathematics and problem solving background and bring more value to the investment decision-making process.

This is my personal website where you can learn more about me and find some examples of work I have done.
