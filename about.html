Introduction

I graduated from North Central College in May of 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and minor in Computer Science. My first two years in college were focused on becoming a high school mathematics teacher. An interest in programming led me to study computer science in addition to mathematics during my final two years.

Before graduating, I was an Assistant Web Developer for my alma mater where I helped redesign and deploy the College's website. In addition, I spent two years as a high school mathematics student teacher as a part of the education program.

This is my personal website where you can find some examples of data analysis work I have done. Areas of interest include option analytics and market data visualization.
