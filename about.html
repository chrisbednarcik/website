Introduction

Since graduating from North Central College in 2020, I have been managing portfolios and performing financial market research with a focus on derivatives and complex options trading strategies. My mathematics and programming education has given me the tools to robustly engage in the world of quantitative finance.

Prior to working in the financial markets, I was an Assistant Web Developer at North Central College where I helped redesign and deploy the College's website. In addition, I spent two years as a high school mathematics student teacher.

This is my personal website where you can find some examples of work I have done. Areas of interest include backtesting performance of complex options trading strategies and analyzing construction of multi-asset portfolios.
